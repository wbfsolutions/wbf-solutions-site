How It Works

Once submitted, your request is reviewed and evaluated based on complexity, scope, and required deliverables. You will receive a response with pricing, timeline, and next steps.

  • Response time: 24–72 hours
  • Clear scope and pricing breakdown provided
  • Revisions discussed before work begins

Pricing Structure

  • Under $100: Full payment upfront
  • $100 – $500: 30% deposit required
  • $500+: 50% deposit required

Work begins only after deposit/payment is received.

Ownership & Usage

Final deliverables are transferred to the client upon full payment. WBF Solutions & Design may display completed work in portfolios unless confidentiality is requested beforehand.
