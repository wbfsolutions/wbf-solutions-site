How It Works

Working with WBF Solutions & Design is a structured, straightforward process focused on delivering usable, production-ready 3D models. From initial concept to final files, each step is designed to keep communication clear and results practical.

Process

1. Project Brief

Send through your idea, sketches, reference images, or existing files. The more detail provided upfront, the more accurately the project can be scoped.

2. Review & Quotation

The project is evaluated based on complexity, detail, and intended use. You’ll receive a clear quote, timeline, and scope before work begins.

3. Design & Development

Models are built with a focus on structure, usability, and real-world application, including manufacturing and fit where required.

4. Feedback & Revisions

Preview versions are shared for review. Adjustments are made based on your feedback to ensure accuracy and alignment with your requirements.

5. Final Delivery

Final files are delivered in the required formats (STL, STEP, OBJ, etc), cleanly structured and ready for use.

What You Can Expect

Clear Communication

Consistent updates and straightforward communication throughout the project.

Production-Ready Models

Files structured for real-world use, not just visual presentation.

Reliable Delivery

Defined timelines and predictable turnaround based on project scope.

Working Internationally

Projects are handled remotely with a focus on clarity and efficiency. Communication is structured to ensure smooth collaboration across time zones.
