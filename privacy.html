Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

WBF Solutions & Design ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and email address
  • Account credentials (handled securely via Firebase Authentication)
  • Profile data (such as display name and avatar)
  • Messages sent through contact forms or email
  • Order and purchase information (processed via third-party providers)
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information

3. How We Collect Information

We collect information when you:

  • Create an account (email/password or Google sign-in)
  • Log into the website
  • Submit forms or contact requests
  • Make purchases through third-party platforms
  • Interact with our website features

4. Authentication & Account Services

We use Firebase Authentication to securely manage user accounts. This includes email/password login and Google OAuth sign-in. We do not store raw passwords on our servers.

5. Data Storage

User data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore). This may include your name, email, account type, and preferences.

6. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Google Firebase – authentication, database, hosting
  • Google OAuth – social login
  • Gumroad – payment processing and digital product delivery

These providers may collect and process your data according to their own privacy policies. We do not control how they process data.

Gumroad Privacy Policy:
https://gumroad.com/privacy

7. Payments

We do not store or process full payment details. All payments are handled securely by Gumroad and its payment processors (such as Stripe or PayPal).

8. Cookies & Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage to:

  • Maintain login sessions
  • Improve user experience
  • Store basic preferences

9. Email Communication

We may send emails related to:

  • Account verification
  • Password resets
  • Important service updates

We do not send unsolicited spam or sell your email address.

10. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections
  • Request deletion of your account and data

To make a request, contact us using the details below.

12. Data Retention

We retain account data as long as your account is active or as required to provide services. You may request deletion at any time.

13. Children’s Privacy

This website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

15. Contact
