About WBF Solutions & Design

Overview

WBF Solutions & Design is a CAD and digital design studio focused on producing practical, production-ready 3D models and digital assets for clients.

The focus is on clear communication, reliable delivery, and work that is usable in real-world applications.

What We Do

We provide CAD modelling, mechanical design support, 3D printable assets, and digital models for prototyping and development workflows.

Approach

Each project is handled with attention to detail, accuracy, and practical application, ensuring the final output meets the intended purpose.

The Meaning Behind the Name

WBF stands for “Walk By Faith” a Christian principle that serves as a personal reminder to honour the Lord in the way the work and business are conducted.

The phrase is drawn from Scripture:

“For we walk by faith, not by sight.” — 2 Corinthians 5:7
